# Parental Leave KPIs

## Companies

Distinct count of companies in the parental leave dataset.

## Industries

Distinct count of industries represented in the dataset.

## Average Paid Maternity Leave

Average number of paid weeks available to mothers after childbirth.

## Average Paid Paternity Leave

Average number of paid weeks available to fathers after childbirth.

## Paid Leave Gap

Difference between average paid maternity leave and average paid paternity leave.
