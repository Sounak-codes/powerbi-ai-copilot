# Parental Leave Glossary

## Company

The company name.

## Industry

The company industry and optional sub-industry.

## Paid Maternity Leave

Paid weeks off from work for mothers after childbirth.

## Unpaid Maternity Leave

Unpaid weeks off from work for mothers after childbirth.

## Paid Paternity Leave

Paid weeks off from work for fathers after childbirth.

## Unpaid Paternity Leave

Unpaid weeks off from work for fathers after childbirth.
